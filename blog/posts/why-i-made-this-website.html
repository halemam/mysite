🔥 Why I Made This Website (Instead of a Resume)

Posted by Sam Alemam · April 16, 2025

Let’s be honest — resumes are dead.

You hand one in, it floats around a few inboxes, maybe gets skimmed (if you’re lucky), and then disappears into some “maybe” folder. Worse? It barely scratches the surface of who you are.

A list of jobs and skills can’t tell you about the person behind the experience — the grind, the growth, the story. And that’s where this website comes in.

I built this as a living resume. A space that speaks for me when I’m not in the room. It’s clean, real, personal — and yeah, way more interesting than a PDF.

This site reflects who I am:

  • 🛠 A creator
  • 🌍 A traveler
  • 🧠 A tech mind
  • 💭 A dreamer with a story worth telling

Every page, every detail — I’ve built it with purpose. Not just to show what I can do, but to show who I am.

Because when someone visits my website, I want them to feel it — not just scroll through it.
