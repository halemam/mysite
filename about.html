Not Your Average About Page

Name’s Sam. I was born in Damascus, Syria. The early days were real — crowded streets, loud nights, and a life that taught me resilience.

I lived in Turkey for a few years. Learned what hustle means. Learned what survival means. Then in 2017, I landed in Canada — no English, no roadmap, just a fire inside and the need to start again.

I taught myself the language. I went back to school. I graduated. I broke into tech. I fell in love with code — not just for the logic, but because it gave me control. A world I could shape. A world I could protect.

Cybersecurity is where I started. But I don’t stop there. I’m building systems. I'm building my name. I’m building a future that’s mine — one line of code, one stretch of road at a time.

Loyal. Sharp. Unshakable.

  • 🚗 I find peace behind the wheel — open road, good music, full tank
  • 🧠 I don't follow trends — I chase mastery
